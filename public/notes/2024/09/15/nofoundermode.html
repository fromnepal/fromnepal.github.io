Work in Your Own Mode

Forget MVPs, SEO, and changing the world. Focus on what matters to you. Market research and user onboarding are often overrated. Productivity is not about being a robot.

Ignore the so-called SaaS gurus and analytics if they don't add value. Avoid getting caught up in social media trends and unnecessary analyses.

Build what you want, how you want. Don't let others dictate your path. Embrace your unique approach and say "no" to distractions.

Big companies have their own challenges. As a startup, you have the freedom to choose your path. Don't follow every trend blindly.

Innovation isn't always necessary. Sometimes, copying and improving existing ideas works just fine. Focus on affordability and practicality.

Don't go "all in" if it means sacrificing your well-being. Have backups and a safety net. Balance your work with personal life.

Build products that are good enough at a lower cost. Compete on price if it makes sense for your business. Enjoy the journey and learn to say "no" more often.

Good luck, and remember to stay true to your vision.
