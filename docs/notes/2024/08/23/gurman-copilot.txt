Apple is planning a major product launch event on September 10, 2024, where it will unveil new iPhones, Apple Watches, and AirPods. The new iPhones are expected to go on sale on September 20. The iPhone 16 will feature larger screens on Pro models and new camera features, while the Apple Watch Series 10 will be thinner with larger screens. New low-end and mid-tier AirPods will include noise cancellation for the first time. Apple is also testing new Mac models with M4 processors, expected to launch later this year.
